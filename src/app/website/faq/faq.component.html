About Career Scripters

How do I order my resume?

To order, you will need to provide your billing and contact information. Then, choose the best-suited package for you.

Why should I get my resume written?

Your resume has to appeal to both the resume scanning software (ATS) and the hiring manager, so, working with a professional resume writer ensures your resume will not be passed over by applicant tracking software. It also makes you more valuable in the eyes of recruiters.

What kind of industries do you specialize in?

Nearly all of them! We have a team of qualified experts with experience across IT, Finance, Business Development, Marketing, Nursing, Healthcare, Education, Computer Engineering, Law Enforcement, Construction, and more from small start-ups to Fortune 500s.

Our Services

Do you tailor resumes and cover letters to specific jobs?

Yes, we sure do! We consider ourselves to be quite versatile with creating resumes and cover letters that resonate with specific jobs of interest. We can also create general ones upon your request.

What resume templates do you offer?

We offer a variety of templates, which are tested and proven to immediately capture the attention of employers. They range from simple, traditional to fancy-style ones with a modern appeal that pass ATS.

How soon can I get my order done?

Effective resumes and cover letters require extensive research of the specific industry and job position you're applying to. Our turnaround time is usually between 2-4 days, depending on the current state of your resume and your requirements. However, we do offer same day delivery at an extra cost.
